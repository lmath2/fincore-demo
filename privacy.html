Privacy

Last updated:

FinCoreApp is a demo that runs entirely in your browser. We don’t have servers and we don’t store your data. Any transactions you enter are kept in your browser’s local storage and disappear if you clear your cache.

What we collect

  • Anonymous usage metrics (page views) via Cloudflare Web Analytics.
  • If you join the waitlist, the Google Form you submit stores your responses in our Google account.
