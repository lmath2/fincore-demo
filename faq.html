FAQ

The most common questions about the demo and what’s next.

What does the demo include?

The demo focuses on a freelancer/sole-prop vertical slice: manual transactions, Profit & Loss, and a simplified Schedule C preview.

Where is my data stored?

Locally in your browser (no server). You can clear or export at any time. For beta cloud accounts, data will be stored securely with strict access controls.

How do Books connect to Taxes?

Each category maps to a tax line. As you add or categorize transactions, the tax preview updates in real time with explanations.

Will you support bank feeds and OCR?

Yes. Planned integrations include Plaid for bank feeds and Textract-style OCR for bills/receipts with human-in-the-loop verification.

When will pricing start?

Beta is free while we build. Final pricing will reflect usage (entities, users, filings) with a generous free tier for small businesses.

How can I give feedback or join the beta?

Use the Feedback button or email lmath@fincoreapp.com. To join the beta, join the waitlist.
